Nutrition Framework
Protein sources to prioritise
Chicken breast, turkey, eggs, Greek yoghurt, cottage cheese, tuna, salmon, lean mince, protein shakes to bridge gaps.
Rules that actually matter
1. Hit protein every day regardless of anything else.
2. Don't pretend the Chinese takeaway didn't happen. Log it.
3. A flat white is ~120 kcal. Fine. Account for it.
4. One bad meal doesn't matter. Three bad days in a row does.
5. Training days: eat toward 2,400. Rest days: eat toward 2,200.
